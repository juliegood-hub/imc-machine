## üìß DISTRIBUTION TIERS (How the IMC Machine Uses This)

### Tier 1: AUTO-SUBMIT (API/Integration)
Every event automatically goes to:
- Eventbrite ‚Üí Google Events
- Facebook Events (via Meta API)
- Evvnt ‚Üí Express-News, MySA, 100+ sites
- Do210 (form submit or partnership)
- Bandsintown/Songkick (music events)

### Tier 2: PRESS RELEASE (Email Blast)
AI-generated press release sent to:
- All TV stations (community calendar desks)
- All radio stations (music/events contacts)
- Express-News, Current, SA Report, SA Magazine
- La Prensa, Out In SA
- Visit San Antonio

### Tier 3: SOCIAL POSTS (API)
Platform-specific AI-generated content posted to:
- Facebook (page post + event)
- Instagram (post + story)
- X/Twitter (tweet + thread)
- LinkedIn (company page)

### Tier 4: PITCH (Manual/AI-Assisted)
AI drafts personalized pitch emails for:
- TV morning show features (Great Day SA, Daytime SA)
- Radio interviews (TPR, KRTU)
- Podcast appearances
- Magazine features (SA Magazine ‚Äî 6-8 week lead)
- Influencer event invites
