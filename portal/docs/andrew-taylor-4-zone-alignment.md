# Andrew Taylor 4-Zone Alignment

This app now includes a structured planning lens based on Andrew Taylor's arts management framing.

Attribution copy used in the product:

`Framework alignment credit: Andrew Taylor (people, money, stuff/place, and purpose/program) adapted for live-event operations.`

## The Four Zones

1. People
Artists, crew, audience, collaborators, and partners.

2. Money
Budgets, ticketing, settlements, payouts, and financial outcomes.

3. Place + Stuff
Venues, zones, inventory, logistics, and technical infrastructure.

4. Purpose + Program
Creative intent, run-of-show, messaging, and program outcomes.

## Production Phases

1. Pre-Production
Plan, build, and align before doors open.

2. Production
Execute live operations and day-of-show performance.

3. Post-Production
Close out settlement, reporting, and learnings.
